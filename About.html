The Origin

Born in Pondicherry,
Built for Speed.

In 2024, our founders realized that while Pondicherry had amazing food—from French bakeries to Chettinad kitchens—getting it delivered was a nightmare. Cold food, late riders, and clunky apps.

We built Food Express to change that. We developed a proprietary AI routing algorithm that predicts traffic patterns in White Town and Heritage Town, ensuring food arrives hot, every time.
